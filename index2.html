The Log is 1 of the 11 cards from Clash Royale to appear in Clash Quest, the others being the Fire Spirit, Bomber, Spear Goblins, Zap, Furnace, Fireball, Goblin Hut, Rocket, Baby Dragon, and Prince. When deployed, you can hear a voice chanting "LOG". The Log can push back a burrowing Miner. Elixir Cost: 2 Arena: Builder's Workshop Release Date: 4 July 2016
