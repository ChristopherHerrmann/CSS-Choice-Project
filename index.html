Clash Royale is a multiplayer action packed card strategy game, requiring the player to make on the spot decisions about when and where to place cards to outplay their oponent and win the game. The game is one by whoever has more crowns at the end of the game, crowns being earned by either destroying the enemys tower or by doing more damage to the oponents tower at the end of overtime. However, an instant win is awarded to a player who can manage to destroy a players king tower, which will instantly end the game. Players can build an upgrade their deck and take it into battle to fight against other Players in the clash royale arena. There are hundreds of cards to choose from, each with there own uniqe mechanic and playstyle assosiated with it. Each player has 8 cards in a deck and each card costs elixer, a recources that builds throughout the game with a maximum of 10 elixer that can be held at once. Each card costs a certain amount of elixer ranging from 1 elixer, all the way up to the most expensive 9 elixer card. Don't let a high elixer cost fool you, as even though it might cost alot, it can easily be killed with smart timing and placment.
